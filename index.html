About

Built to ask why.

I'm a product manager and systems thinker. The analytical instinct doesn't turn off. A few years ago, waiting at a pizza place, a friend and I started wondering how many pizzas they sell in a day. By the time we reached the cashier, we'd modeled line length, peak windows, the whole-to-slice ratio, and a day-of-week adjustment, then asked the person at the register for the actual number. That's not a professional exercise. That's just how my brain works.

How I work

I move between strategy and execution without losing the thread. I'll write a product spec in the morning and dig into a SQL query in the afternoon. Most product problems are actually coordination problems: the wrong people don't have the right information at the right time. I'm good at fixing that, and good at being the person in the room who asks the question everyone else assumed had already been answered.

Bilingual (English / Spanish), background across strategy consulting, operations, and product. I've spent time on both sides of the table: delivering the product and using it to do a job. That shapes how I think about what actually matters.
